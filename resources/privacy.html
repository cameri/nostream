Privacy Policy

What we collect

When you connect to this relay, we may collect and retain the following information:

  • Your IP address, for the purpose of rate limiting, abuse detection, and spam prevention.
  • Your Nostr public key, if you publish events or request an admission invoice.
  • Events you publish, including their content, timestamps, and signatures, which are stored and distributed to other clients and relays per the Nostr protocol.
How we use it
  • To provide the relay service.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, spam, and misuse.
  • Your IP address and/or public key, in full, truncated, or as a hash digest, may be shared with other relays and clients solely for abuse reporting purposes.
What we do not do
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
Data retention

Events you publish are stored and may be retained indefinitely. Requests to delete events you have published cannot be guaranteed, as copies may exist on other relays in the Nostr network.

Third-party services

This relay may use third-party services (such as Lightning payment processors) to facilitate payments. Those services have their own privacy policies which govern their data practices.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated at any time without explicit notice. Continued use of the relay constitutes acceptance of the current policy.
